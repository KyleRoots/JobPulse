## Cleaned-up version

Here is a tighter, fact‑aligned rewrite based on current info:

- GPT‑4o was removed from the main ChatGPT interface on February 13, 2026, alongside several other older models, as OpenAI shifted usage toward newer GPT‑5‑series models.[1][4][6][8]

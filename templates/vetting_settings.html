How It Works
1
Detection

Every 3 minutes, the system checks for new applicants — new candidates are typically vetted within minutes of applying

2
Resume Analysis

GPT-4o extracts and analyzes the candidate's resume against all open positions in monitored tearsheets

3
Match Scoring

Each candidate-job pair receives a compatibility score based on skills, experience, and qualifications

4
Note Creation

A detailed note is added to every candidate record with the analysis results

5
Recruiter Alerts

For candidates scoring {{ settings.match_threshold }}%+, the assigned recruiter receives an email notification

Job Requirements

Per-job AI requirements, custom overrides, and thresholds are managed from the Scout Screening page. Use the buttons above to re-run AI extraction across all active jobs or re-sync the job list from Bullhorn.

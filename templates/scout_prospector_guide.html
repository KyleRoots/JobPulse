Welcome to Scout Prospector

Scout Prospector uses AI and real-time web search to discover ideal client companies for your staffing business. Build an Ideal Client Profile (ICP), run AI research, and get a list of qualified prospects complete with key contacts and engagement signals.

Getting Started — Quick Workflow

1

Create an ICP Profile — Define who your ideal client is: industries, company sizes, locations, job types you staff for, and what hiring signals matter to you.

2

Run AI Research — Click the "Research" button on your ICP profile. The AI searches the web in real time and returns a list of companies matching your criteria.

3

Review Prospects — Browse your discovered prospects, check qualification scores, view key contacts with LinkedIn profiles, and mark their status as you engage.

4

Export & Act — Export your prospects to CSV for outreach, or manage them directly from the dashboard by updating statuses and adding notes.

Creating an ICP Profile

The ICP (Ideal Client Profile) wizard walks you through four steps. Each step focuses on a different aspect of your target client:

Step 1 — Profile Basics: Give your profile a name and a detailed description. The description is important because the AI uses it to understand exactly what kind of companies you're looking for.

AI Suggest Criteria: After writing your description, click "AI Suggest Criteria" to have the AI recommend industries, company sizes, locations, job types, and signals based on what you wrote. You can apply the suggestions to pre-fill the remaining steps, then fine-tune as needed.

Step 2 — Target Industries: Check the industries you want to focus on. Use "Select All" if your services span multiple sectors.

Step 3 — Geography & Roles: Specify the locations and job types you typically staff for. This helps the AI find companies in the right markets hiring for the right roles.

Step 4 — Hiring Signals & Review: Choose which signals matter (active job postings, recent funding, growth, etc.) and add any extra criteria. Review your full profile summary before saving.

Running AI Research

From the dashboard, click the Research button next to any ICP profile. The AI will search the web in real time, looking for companies that match your profile criteria.

Research typically takes 30–60 seconds. The AI evaluates multiple sources, checks company details, and assigns a qualification score (0–100%) based on how closely each company matches your ICP.

You can run research multiple times on the same profile — each run may discover new companies. Previously found companies won't be duplicated.

Managing Prospects

Each discovered prospect includes the company name, industry, estimated size, location, qualification score, and key contacts. Click on any prospect to see full details including:

Key Contacts: Names, titles, LinkedIn URLs, and public email addresses when available. Click LinkedIn links to view profiles directly.

Source URLs: The web pages where the AI found information about this company.

Notes: Add your own notes to track outreach attempts, call outcomes, or follow-up reminders.

Status When to Use
New Freshly discovered — not yet reviewed or contacted
Hot High-priority prospect — strong match, ready for immediate outreach
Warm Good match — worth following up, but not urgent
Cold Low priority — keep on file for future consideration
Contacted You've reached out — awaiting response
Not Interested Prospect declined or doesn't fit — archived from active view

Exporting Data

Click Export CSV from the dashboard to download your prospects. The export includes company details, contacts (with LinkedIn URLs and emails), qualification scores, statuses, and notes. Use filters first to export only the prospects you need.

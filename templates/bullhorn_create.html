How to Find Your Tearsheet ID
  1. Log into your Bullhorn system
  2. Navigate to the tearsheet you want to monitor
  3. Look at the URL in your browser's address bar
  4. The tearsheet ID is the number after "TearsheetID=" or similar parameter

Example URL: https://app.bullhorn.com/BullhornWeb/Tearsheet.cfm?TearsheetID=12345
